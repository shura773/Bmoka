Our Story

Why we make what we make.

A small team obsessed with getting the details right, so you don't have to think twice about the gear you rely on.

Core Products

MyShop started in a garage with one question: why does most everyday gear feel disposable? We set out to make a small line of products that hold up — worn in, not worn out.

Every item goes through the same process: a working prototype, months of real use, and a redesign before it ever reaches the catalog. If it doesn't earn its place in daily rotation, it doesn't ship.

We're still a small team. That means slower growth, but it also means every product carries the same standard — no shortcuts, no filler.
